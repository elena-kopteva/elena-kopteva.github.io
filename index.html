About

I am a researcher at the University of Illinois Urbana‑Champaign (UIUC). My main areas of interest are theoretical cosmology, general relativity, and astrophysical models of cosmic expansion. I also have a deep commitment to teaching and mentoring students in physics and mathematics.

Research

Black‑hole solutions with exotic matter

Exploring anisotropic fluids and cosmic‑string gases in GR.

I study modifications of black‑hole spacetimes embedded in non‑trivial matter distributions and their physical implications.

Cosmic expansion models

Non‑uniform pressure and anisotropies in cosmology.

Analyzing the role of non‑uniform pressures in driving cosmic acceleration, connecting models to observational data.
